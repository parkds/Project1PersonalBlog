About


Masters of Science in Computer Science

Hi, my name is David Park! I got my Masters of Science in Computer Science from Georgia Tech in May 2020.

My concentration is in Machine Learning, however I took courses from each of the other specialization such as Computing Systems, Robotics and Perception, and Interactive Intelligence for a well rounded CS education.

During my time at Georgia Tech, I worked on a 8803 special problems project along with 3 other team members to perform independent research. Our paper was accepted at an ACM conference, where we will be presenting our research in August 2020. I also had the pleasure to work as a part time graduate teaching assistant for one of the Machine Learning specialization courses. I am proud to continue supporting the program as an Instructional Associate for the Summer 2020 semester.

Have I mentioned that this was all done part time while working? The OMSCS program allows motivated stuents to pursue their graudate education while pursuing their full time jobs and or other family responsibilities. I am proud to parttake in this transformative effort that enables a quality graduate level education to those around the world.

This blog is dedicated to sharing my experiences during my OMSCS journey. I will be leaving my thoughts on the courses I took, and also share tips and guidelines for those who maybe embarking on their MSCS journey.
